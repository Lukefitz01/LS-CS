Universal Design

My website includes Equitable use by making the design appealing to all users by the button and navbar styling

My website includes Flexibility in use by haveing padding on the input fields and centering the form to accoomdate for left and right handed use

My website includes Simple and Intuitive Use by providing effective prompting and feedback during and after task completion, most notably when input fields and missing an alert pops up with what field is not filled in

My website includes Perceptible Information as all information is legible and not obstructed by a background colour that would have made the text unreadable or hard to read

My Website includes Tolerance for Error by providing warnings of hazards and errors, along with providing fail-safe features. If a user forgets to input a field , an alert pops up telling them what field they have forgotten . If a credit card num is entered incorrectly , the website checks that also and implements an alert to tell the user

My website includes Low physical effort by minimising sustained phyisical effort by reducing the length of the form by putting some input fields beside each other and bot every input field in a straight line

My website includes Size and Space for Approach and Use by making all components comfortable for any seated of standing user. This is implemented by padding on the input fields to make them easier to click along with a big submit button
